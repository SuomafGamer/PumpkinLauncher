How to Play
1) Choose your angle:
Tap Click/drag on the screen to choose your launch angle.

2) Choose your power:
Tap Click confirm to launch the pumpkin with the percentage of power displayed on the gauge.

3) Boost your pumpkin:
Tap Click/drag to control your pumpkin while airborne. You have limited fuel (displayed on the left of the screen); pickups may be collected to replenish this.
